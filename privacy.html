Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Nova AI Studio collects, uses, and protects information when you use the mobile application Rental Expense Keeper.

Information We Collect

Rental Expense Keeper may process receipt images, expense details, property names, categories, amounts, dates, and notes that you enter or upload in the app.

Receipt Image and AI Processing

When you use the AI receipt scanning feature, receipt images may be uploaded to our server and processed by an AI service to extract expense information. Receipt images are used only to provide the receipt analysis feature.

Local Storage

Expense records and property information may be stored locally on your device. You can delete properties and expenses from within the app.

How We Use Information

We use information only to provide and improve app features, including:

  • Scanning and analyzing receipts
  • Organizing rental property expenses
  • Displaying expense history, totals, and reports
  • Maintaining app functionality and reliability

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Receipt images and related data may be processed by trusted service providers only as needed to provide app functionality, such as cloud hosting and AI analysis.

Data Retention

Receipt images uploaded for AI analysis are intended to be processed only for analysis purposes. Locally stored records remain on your device unless you delete them or uninstall the app.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

Rental Expense Keeper is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
